About Chair Islamic TV

Assalamu Alaikum. Welcome to Chair Islamic TV — your companion for learning Qur'an, Hadith, and Islamic knowledge. We provide Yasarnah Qur'an lessons, authentic Hadith from Arba'un Nawawi, prayer times, and Qibla direction. Our mission is to make Islamic education accessible to every Muslim in Uganda and beyond.
